Selected work 01 · Independent project · 2026

XR teleoperation for the Unitree G1

I built a Meta Quest interface that maps an operator’s arm and hand motion onto a simulated G1 humanoid. The system connects a Unity digital twin to a WSL2 robotics backend for dual-arm inverse kinematics and Dex3 hand retargeting.

What it demonstrates

Controller and optical hand input can drive a 29-DoF humanoid through a complete simulated pick, transport, and place task. Physical robot output remains deliberately disabled.

My contribution

  1. 01

    MotionCoordinate-frame calibration, dual-arm IK, wrist orientation, joint limits, and recovery states.

  2. 02

    HandsQuest landmark capture, Dex3 retargeting, filtering, and controller grasp synergies.

  3. 03

    InteractionUDP transport, tracking-loss safety, contact-qualified grasping, slip, and release.
